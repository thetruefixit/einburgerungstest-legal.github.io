2. General Information on Data Processing

We take the protection of your personal data very seriously. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

Principles of Our Data Processing

  • Data Minimization: We only collect data necessary for app functionality
  • Local Storage: Your learning data is primarily stored locally on your device
  • Consent: External data transmission only occurs with your explicit consent
  • Transparency: Complete information about all data processing activities

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

6.1 Fundamental Rights

  • Right of Access (Art. 15 GDPR): Information about processed data
  • Right to Rectification (Art. 16 GDPR): Correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure (Art. 17 GDPR): "Right to be forgotten"
  • Right to Restriction (Art. 18 GDPR): Limitation of processing
  • Right to Data Portability (Art. 20 GDPR): Export of your data
  • Right to Object (Art. 21 GDPR): Objection to processing

6.2 Withdrawal of Consent

You can withdraw your consent for analytics and crash reporting at any time:

  • In the App: Settings → Privacy → Disable Analytics/Crash Reporting
  • By Email: dabeapps@gmail.com

Withdrawal only affects future processing and does not affect the lawfulness of processing prior to withdrawal.

6.3 Data Export and Deletion

Functions available in the app:

  • Data Export: Settings → "Export Data"
  • Delete Local Data: Settings → "Clear All Data"
  • Uninstall App: Automatically removes all local data

7.2 Organizational Measures

  • Regular security reviews
